Yikai Wang 王毅楷

I am a senior undergraduate student at the Weiyang College, Tsinghua University, where my major is Mechanical Engineering, as well as Mathematics and Physics.

I am a student intern at Carnegie Mellon University and a reserch assistant at the ISR Lab, IIIS, Tsinghua University. I am honored to be advised by Prof. Guanya Shi, Prof.Jianyu Chen, and Prof.Ding Zhao.

My research objective is to enhance the robot's motion capabilities, enabling it to perform high-dynamic tasks while ensuring safety. Building upon this, the robot can accomplish more versatile tasks in complex environments. To this end, I think that reinforcement learning and principles from control theory could be feasible approaches.

I am seeking PhD positions beginning Fall 2024!
